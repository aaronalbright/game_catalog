How to enter a game.

  1. Look at the box. The name of the game is probably on the front it big letters.
  2. Look at the box again. The console the game is for is probably at the top. (It awalys is)
  3. If there is no sleeve (or no box), look at the disk and refer to steps 1 and 2.
  4. Ask the customer how much they paid for their game.
  5. Offer them half of half that price.
  6. Enjoy the look of confusion and sadness rush over the customers face.
  7. Answer any and all questions with "That's just what my manager tells me." Even if you're the manager.
  8. Take the game and give the customer their money.
  9. DON'T forget to get the customers initials.

Congratulations! You just made GamesGalor Inc. money. Now get to work.
